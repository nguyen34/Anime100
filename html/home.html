Project Concept

Think back to your first encounter with Japanese anime and consider how you keep developing your relationship with Japanese anime

Venue

Speech contest will be held online. Our official website will run everything, including voting.

Period

November 30th ~ December 20th

Candidates

Seventeen students, who are selected from each university which are all highly recognized in Japan:

Canada: UBC, Toronto

Western America：Stanford University California Institute of Technology University of California, Berkeley University of Southern California University of California, Los Angeles University of California, Santa Barbara University of California, Irvine

Eastern America : Brown University, Columbia University, Harvard University, the University of Pennsylvania, Princeton University, Yale University, Chicago, MIT
