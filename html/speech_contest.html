  • Speech Theme Encounter

    • Think back on your own past, appreciate an encounter and think about your own future, how a work changed you and what you want to convey from your experience.

  • Evaluation Criteria

    • Structure, Creativity, Persuasion, Clear, Coherent

  • Tips for Making a Speech

    • What made you to be an anime fan, how anime influenced you, episode of your turning point, What anime means to you, how you want Japanese anime to develop in the future, Comparison Japanese anime and other countries’ anime, how Japanese anime is accepted differently in Japan and North America, episode of visiting a real-life location considering tourism and economic impact,　As a work gave you dream or hope, you want to do the same thing to others, which character’s belief you introduced in your everyday life, the reason why you keep loving a character or a work as you become older,Which work saved your life, You story that a work made you decide your major,　what you thought after taking a course learning Japanese subculture （anime） in university, why you like fiction especially anime instead of other media such as novels or manga, Which work changed your relationship with others in real life, the reason why you want to keep watching anime after you start working, you want to watch anime your future kids just as you were impressed etc.
